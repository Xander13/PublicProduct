Vigor

A platform that suggest workouts that adapts to the user's environment and schedule.

Process Deck

Client: School Project

Role: GUI Design

Duration: 12 weeks

Task

Create an interactive system to solve personal training exercise routines.

Content analysis

Low Visual Hierarchy

Some users struggle or are not interested in reading through too much information. They simply just want to get started.

Bad Viewing

Viewing excel sheets is not friendly on mobile.

Discoverability

Users want real-time response of progress and guidance as to what they are doing or should be doing. Instantaneous self-gratification.






How can we create a fitness design system for busy Americans to meet federal fitness guidelines?

Research

I have conducted interviews with many students about their experiences at the gym. The research helps me understand the users' needs of my app creation.

I learned that being in a place like a gym can be hard for students because they get the feeling of gymtimidation.

I also learned that students like to be in their own space when they work out, such as doing their things at home or creating hobbies like biking to help get their workout done.

Goals



Personalization

Bring fitness into an everyday life routine. Avoid the need to go only to the gym to workout.

Achieving

Making the work out process easier.

Motivating

Making things more attainable for the user to workout.

Convenient

Workout without needing to go to the gym.

Comfortable

Reducing the friction of gymtimidation.





Busy Beth

As a busy mom, I wish to maintain a healthy level of stress to keep my body in shape. I wish to find an workout app that will fit my busy life taking care of my kids.

Scenario

Beth wanted to start exercising without having to go to the gym. She wanted to owrk out where she feels comfortable such as home.

Expectations

- Easy flow app that gets Beth what she is looking for.

- Versatile workouts whereever she is at.

- Keeps her motivated to return to the app.

Homepage

I wanted the user to have quick access to the workout the app recommended to easily earn their points they need to fulfill for the week.

I also have a custom user status on how many points they have collected or they wish to add other things for their workout focus.

Status Page

Knowing how much the user has done for the week can help keep them at a good pace.

Americans tend to start a workout but end up being burnt out and quit. I wanted to improve that and get them in a consistent workout routine.
